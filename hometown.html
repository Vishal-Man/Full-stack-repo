My Home Town

HYDERABAD

History & Culture: Founded centuries ago, it was a significant center for trade and rulers, evident in its architecture and traditions.

Economy: A major IT, BPO, and pharmaceutical hub, driving significant growth.

Cuisine: World-renowned for its Mughlai and Hyderabadi dishes, with Biriyani being a signature item.

Landmarks: Home to iconic sites like Charminar, Golconda Fort, and the Hussain Sagar Lake.

Nickname: Known as the "City of Pearls" due to its historical pearl trade.
